# Project Brief: TrackFlow

## Business Context
TrackFlow is a last-mile delivery and warehouse management company operating in the US and Spain, serving e-commerce brands. Founded in 2009, it employs ~130 people and generates ~€9M in annual revenue. The company manages warehousing, last-mile delivery, and reverse logistics for its clients, allowing brands to focus on sales while TrackFlow handles logistics.

## Problem Statement
TrackFlow's current systems are fragmented: two warehouses use different systems, carrier management is manual, returns are handled case-by-case, and customer queries are answered manually. The lack of unified infrastructure leads to inefficiency, errors, and slow decision-making.

## Mandate
The TrackFlow Tech unit is tasked with building the systems, integrations, and automations needed to modernize operations, unify data, and enable scalable, efficient logistics across both countries.
